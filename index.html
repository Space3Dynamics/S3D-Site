Our mission

We are a team with experience in nanosatellites, control systems, propulsion and development of subsystems that begins under the auspices of the SUCHAI 2 & 3 Project of the University of Chile. We have been trained in simulation of the space environment at the University of Tokyo, Japan thanks to the SEELA program. Our objective is:

  • Sell relatively low-cost products through a recycled manufacturing model.
  • Serve quality services with reports of various simulations.
  • Supply educational training based on experience.

Currently, our team members are part of the PlantSat project, an international project in collaboration with RocketLab and Gomspace.

Education

Check our Educational Services

Our team is committed to space education.

We offer educational services in the format of courses for High School* and University students. The courses are:

  • Control and estimation of spacecraft orientation.
  • Orbital dynamics control, estimation sensors and thrusters.
  • Compressible flow fundamentals.
  • Tutorial course to use free development simulators.

* Some services may be free for high schools. You can find free projects here .
