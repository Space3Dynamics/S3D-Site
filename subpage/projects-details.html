Spacecraft Simulator.

We are working on the development of a space vehicle simulator in collaboration with the SPEL laboratory. The simulator consists of the following features:

  • Dynamics of celestial bodies (JPL kernels).
  • Components simulation (Reaction wheel, Gyroscope, Magnetorquer, Sun sensors, etc).
  • Electric power subsystem in operation (Solar panel, battery, EPS).
  • Integration of online estimation and control algorithms.
  • Simulation report in .CSV file.
